Privacy Policy for Streak Seeker

Last updated: February 13, 2026

Data Collection

Streak Seeker does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All habit tracking data is stored locally on your device using browser localStorage.

Data Storage

All data remains on your device and is never sent to any external servers. Your habits, completions, and streaks are completely private.

Third-Party Services

Streak Seeker does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

Permissions

Streak Seeker does not request any browser permissions beyond basic extension functionality.
